The Studio

Misterio Color Lab is a post-production boutique specializing in color grading and finishing. We believe in the power of color to tell stories, conveying the exact emotion in every frame.

Our approach combines cutting-edge technology with a unique artistic vision, working closely with directors and cinematographers to achieve their ultimate vision.
